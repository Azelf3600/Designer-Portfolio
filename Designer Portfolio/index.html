The Scribe's Ledger

I began my freelance illustration and design journey in 2021, driven by a passion for visual storytelling and a need to forge my own path. Since then, my work has crossed borders, allowing me to collaborate closely with creators worldwide on long-term passion projects.

My portfolio spans a diverse range of creative realms: from designing comic characters and sequential strips for a novelist in Europe, to creating vibrant digital content for a Minecraft creator in Australia. Most notably, I have spent the last three years collaborating with a dedicated client to bring immersive tabletop campaigns to life through custom fantasy artwork. I specialize in turning complex world-building into striking visual realities.
